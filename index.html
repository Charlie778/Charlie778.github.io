My experience in eight valley class

Nowadays,i attend a project called Eight Valley which is a project about the new retail .This a project which contains a roadshow to show the strangers what we learn during the summer vacation.So far,I have taken part in three online courses,I have learn some key points about the profit and some characteristics of the new retail.Before that,the teacher let us to search for the history of the Squatter communities by ourselves ,this can help us find the earliest Squatter communities,and make the compare between the common retail and the new retail easier.After the self-research,i found that it needs some new technologies for common retail to develop to the new retail.These technologies contains special vending machines and these vending machines should have some uncommon features like no labor is needed for this machine to work.Besides this, the teacher also divided us into small departments like data analyst,science and technology personnel,interaction designer and the product manager.We have different duties and responsibilities.I feel good after taking these courses.

My first experience in Reading Seminar

Before I attend the reading seminar, I feel quite confused about the reason why this kind of meeting is held. My classmates told me that this meeting is a platform to share the feeling after reading a book. Like the saying goes there are a thousand Hamlets in a thousand eyes. People always have different thoughts after reading a same book. This small meeting gives us a small chance to share our ideas about one book. Then I take part In that meeting. First time I feel quite nervous and I feel hard to get the point they share. But after some time, I get used to the steps and the point. Then I tried to share my own point of view of a book. For example, I shared with my classmates a book called 1984.It is a great book of telling a story about people who follows every suggestions given by the Big Brother. That is a dystopian book. I feel really nervous since it is the first time I share my point of view in this kinds of meeting. I think I may be better later.

the review of an article

From the author’s point of view, he thinks keeping young children in quarantine time is
quite tougher than any other time. (Moyer, M. W. 2020, June 9th, The Do’s and Don’ts of
‘Quarantine Pods’) Since it is impossible for children to play as usual in quarantine time, people
get an idea of making a pod to ensure children’s basic playing time. The same as other pods,
other families also struggle with the lack of communication. As a result, most children get good
playing time during quarantine time. Since the author thinks young children need more physical
playing than any other kinds of playing, it’s quite hard to prevent their children from keeping in
touch with others in reality. (Moyer, M. W. 2020, June 9th, The Do’s and Don’ts of ‘Quarantine
Pods’) This is one of the reasons why the author thinks it’s hard to keep young children during
quarantine time. The author also considers that the working memory of children is poorer than
other ages, so it’s tough for them to keep the rules in their minds. (Moyer, M. W. 2020, June 9th,
The Do’s and Don’ts of ‘Quarantine Pods’) So the adults must look after them every time they
get out to have fun. If they do not do so, their children will break the rules. Besides these the
author also thinks making children play with masks has high risk of being safe because of the
possibility of kids via the air they share. Another important reason is it’s difficult to trace the
origin if one family got a sick person because of the large range of the children playing space. All
in all, the author list four different reasons to support his opinion. Also,he get information from
some professors and himself.
