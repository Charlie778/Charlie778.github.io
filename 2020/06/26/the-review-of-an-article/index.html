the review of an article

From the author’s point of view, he thinks keeping young children in quarantine time is
quite tougher than any other time. (Moyer, M. W. 2020, June 9th, The Do’s and Don’ts of
‘Quarantine Pods’) Since it is impossible for children to play as usual in quarantine time, people
get an idea of making a pod to ensure children’s basic playing time. The same as other pods,
other families also struggle with the lack of communication. As a result, most children get good
playing time during quarantine time. Since the author thinks young children need more physical
playing than any other kinds of playing, it’s quite hard to prevent their children from keeping in
touch with others in reality. (Moyer, M. W. 2020, June 9th, The Do’s and Don’ts of ‘Quarantine
Pods’) This is one of the reasons why the author thinks it’s hard to keep young children during
quarantine time. The author also considers that the working memory of children is poorer than
other ages, so it’s tough for them to keep the rules in their minds. (Moyer, M. W. 2020, June 9th,
The Do’s and Don’ts of ‘Quarantine Pods’) So the adults must look after them every time they
get out to have fun. If they do not do so, their children will break the rules. Besides these the
author also thinks making children play with masks has high risk of being safe because of the
possibility of kids via the air they share. Another important reason is it’s difficult to trace the
origin if one family got a sick person because of the large range of the children playing space. All
in all, the author list four different reasons to support his opinion. Also,he get information from
some professors and himself.
